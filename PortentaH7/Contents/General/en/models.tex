\section{Models}

When installing NVidia's jetson-inference\index{jetson-inference} project, the utility offers a large selection of pre-trained deep learning models for download. Among them are well-known ones like the AlexNet{AlexNet} from 2012 as well as various so-called \ac{resnets}. Also included are SSD-Mobilenet-V2\index{SSD-Mobilenet-V2}, which recognises 90 objects from apples to toothbrushes, and DeepScene\index{DeepScene} from the University of Freiburg for recognising objects in road traffic. 

To download more models, the Model Downloader can be called up:

\medskip

\SHELL{\$ cd jetson-inference/tools} \newline 
\SHELL{\$ ./download-models.sh} 

\medskip

The models  GoogleNet and ResNet-18, which are based on the ImageNet\index{ImageNet} image database, are automatically downloaded in the build step. The table~\ref{Database:TrainedModel} lists all models that are available in the jetson-inference project. The first column of the table is the name of the structure of the model. \cite{Alom:2018}. The second column contains the passing parameter for the \PYTHON{--network} argument of the \FILE{imagenet-camera.py} program.

\medskip

    \centering

        GoogleNet\index{GoogleNet}    & googlenet             & GOOGLENET \\
        GoogleNet-12                  & googlenet-12          & GOOGLENET\_12 \\
        ResNet-18\index{ResNets}      & resnet-18             & RESNET\_18 \\

    \end{tabular}
